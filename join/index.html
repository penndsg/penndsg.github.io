Join Our Mailing List

We send out regular emails to notify data-oriented students of events or opportunities that may be of interest. Click here to join. Here’s a sample of the types of things we put in these emails:

  • Upcoming PDSG events (and other group events)
  • Job opportunities
  • Data bootcamps (Data Incubator, Insight Data Science)
  • Datathons
  • Local meetups (DataPhilly, PhillyPUG)

Like Us On Facebook

All of our events are also posted on our Facebook page! Like us to get notifications about upcoming events.

Join Us On Slack

We have a growing community of data-oriented students on Slack. Anyone with a UPenn email address can join our community by following this link. Our Slack community is a great place to meet other students with similar interests, get technical help, and get career advice. You can talk about whatever you want here, as long as you are respectful of others.

Email Us

If you would like to ask us a question or contribute in any way to PDSG, plese email us at board@penndsg.com, and we’ll respond as soon as we can!
